# MediMind AI

## Software Requirement Specification (SRS)

## Project Title

MediMind AI – Intelligent Voice-Based Medication Assistant

---

## Problem Statement

Many people forget to take medicines on time, especially elderly people and patients taking multiple medicines. Existing reminder applications mainly provide alarms but lack intelligent features such as voice interaction, AI assistance, prescription scanning, and medication tracking.

---

## Project Objective

To develop an AI-powered medication assistant that reminds users to take medicines on time, accepts voice commands, scans prescriptions, provides medicine-related information, and tracks medication adherence.

---

## Target Users

- Elderly people
- Busy professionals
- Patients taking multiple medicines
- Caregivers
- Family members

---

## Main Features

- User Registration
- User Login
- Medicine Management
- Voice Reminder
- Voice Medicine Entry
- AI Chatbot
- Prescription Scanner
- Medication Tracking
- Daily Reports
- Weekly Reports
- Missed Dose Detection
- Smart Notifications
